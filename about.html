Background & History

Bloemfontein Community Support (BCS) was officially founded on 05 August 2025 by local volunteers who recognised community needs. We run food distribution, community gardens, tutoring and skills training.

Mission

To empower underprivileged communities in Bloemfontein through education and food security programmes.

Vision

Thriving, resilient communities with access to education, food and opportunity.
